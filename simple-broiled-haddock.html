Simple Broiled Haddock


This honey chicken recipe was given to me by a friend. I've used this recipe for
years, and my family requests it often. It's a light meal that everyone will enjoy.
Serve with steamed rice.

Prep Time:

10 mins		 Cook Time:

10 mins		 Total Time:

20 mins
Servings:

4

Ingredients


  • 2 pounds haddock fillets

  • ½ teaspoon onion powder

  • ½ teaspoon paprika

  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder

  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper

  • ½ teaspoon salt

  • ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper

  • 1 tablespoon butter, cut in small pieces

  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges

Directions


Step 1

Gather all ingredients.


Step 2

Set an oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's
broiler. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil; spray with cooking spray.


Step 3

Arrange haddock fillets on the prepared baking sheet.


Step 4

Mix onion powder, paprika, garlic powder, black pepper, salt, and cayenne
pepper in a small bowl; sprinkle seasoning over fish, then dot with butter.


Step 5

Broil in the preheated oven until fish flakes easily with a fork, 6 to 8 minutes.


Step 6

Serve with lemon wedges. Enjoy!
